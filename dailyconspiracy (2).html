Tupac is alive?


This week we will look at a conspiracy theory that is a hot topic and very popular today regarding the famous rapper/pop culture icon Tupac Shakur. Oficial records state that Tupac died on September 13, 1996, at the age of 25, from gunshot wounds suffered seven days earlier.Here is the official story of Tupac's death. After he was killed, Tupac's mother supposedly had him cremated. While there are many conspiracies relating to who killed Tupac and his motives, today we will look at the conspiracy stating that he faked his death.
One of the main pieces of evidence that Tupac is still alive is his obsession with Machiavelli, an Italian war strategist. Tupac read all of his books and even referred to himself as Makavelli. In his book, Machiavelli said a clever way to fool your enemies is to fake your own death. It can be seen that Tupac could take that to heart, possibly to avoid prison for previous charges. After his death, Tupac's last album released, titled "The Don Kiluminati: The 7 Day Theory." This can be easily percieved as a reference to the seven days that it took him to die after he was wounded. There are also many lyrical refrenced to the theory. Also, the murderer was never found, even in a city like Las Vegas which is surrounded by desert. Finally, music producer Suge Knight claims that he is alive.
